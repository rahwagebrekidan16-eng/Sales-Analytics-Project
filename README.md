# Sales-Analytics-Project
A sales analytics project using Python and Excel Dashboard to analyze sales trends and generate business insights.
## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes an Amazon sales dataset to identify sales trends, top-performing products, customer purchasing patterns, and regional sales performance. The analysis was conducted using Python for data cleaning, exploration, and visualization, while Microsoft Excel was used to develop an interactive sales dashboard.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Clean and prepare sales data for analysis.
- Analyze monthly and yearly sales performance.
- Identify top-performing product categories and brands.
- Evaluate sales performance across different states.
- Discover sales trends and seasonal patterns.
- Develop an interactive Excel dashboard.
- Provide business recommendations based on the analysis.

## 📈 Key Findings

- **Total Sales:** 91,825,647.92
- **Highest Sales State:** Texas (TX)
- **Lowest Sales State:** Arizona (AZ)
- **Highest Revenue Category:** Electronics
- **Highest Monthly Sales:** May 2024
- Sales remained relatively stable throughout the five-year period with normal monthly fluctuations.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Sales in Low-Performing States

Arizona recorded the lowest sales performance. Amazon can increase sales by implementing targeted marketing campaigns, regional promotions, and customer engagement strategies.

### 2. Expand High-Performing Categories

Electronics generated the highest sales revenue. Increasing inventory, expanding product offerings, and promoting related accessories can further improve profitability.

### 3. Optimize Seasonal Planning

Since sales peak during certain months such as May, Amazon should prepare inventory, marketing campaigns, and logistics ahead of high-demand periods while introducing promotional offers during slower months.
